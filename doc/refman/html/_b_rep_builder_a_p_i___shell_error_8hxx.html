enum BRepBuilderAPI_ShellError

Indicates the outcome of the construction of a face, i.e. whether it is successful or not, as explained below:

  • BRepBuilderAPI_ShellDone No error occurred. The shell is correctly built.
  • BRepBuilderAPI_EmptyShell No initialization of the algorithm: only an empty constructor was used.
  • BRepBuilderAPI_DisconnectedShell not yet used
  • BRepBuilderAPI_ShellParametersOutOfRange The parameters given to limit the surface are out of its bounds.
