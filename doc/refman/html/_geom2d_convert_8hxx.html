class  Geom2dConvert
 This package provides an implementation of algorithmes to do the conversion between equivalent geometric entities from package Geom2d. It gives the possibility : . to obtain the B-spline representation of bounded curves. . to split a B-spline curve into several B-spline curves with some constraints of continuity, . to convert a B-spline curve into several Bezier curves or surfaces. All the geometric entities used in this package are bounded. References : . Generating the Bezier Points of B-spline curves and surfaces (Wolfgang Bohm) CAGD volume 13 number 6 november 1981 .
